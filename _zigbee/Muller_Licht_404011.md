## Tasmota2Zigbee

You need to listen to the pre-configured group addresses 0x4004, 0x4005, 0x4006 corresponding to the 3 lights.

Example: `backlog zblisten1 0x4004; zblisten2 0x04005; zblisten3 0x4006`
